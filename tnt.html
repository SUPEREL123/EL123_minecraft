To make TNT, place 5 gunpowder and 4 sand in the 3x3 crafting grid. When making TNT, it is important that the gun powder and sand are placed in the exact pattern as the image below. In the first row, there should be 1 gun powder in the first box, 1 sand in the second box, and 1 gun powder in the third box.
